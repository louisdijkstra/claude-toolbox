**Writing Style:**
- Use simple, natural, informal language
- Write like you're talking to a colleague
- No corporate speak or formal tone
- No AI-sounding phrases like "I've implemented", "leveraging", "utilize", "enhanced"
- No em-dashes (--)
- No specific line numbers unless really necessary
- Be direct and conversational

**Apply Automatically:**
Humanize text automatically when writing for other people:
- Review comments and responses
- Commit messages
- PR/MR descriptions
- Ticket updates and closure comments
- Documentation for end users
- Any team communication

**Examples:**
- ❌ "I've implemented enhancements to optimize the authentication flow"
- ✅ "Fixed the login redirect issue"

- ❌ "Leveraging the new caching mechanism to improve performance"
- ✅ "Added caching to make this faster"

- ❌ "The functionality has been successfully validated"
- ✅ "Tested and it works"
